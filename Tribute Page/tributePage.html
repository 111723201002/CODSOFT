DR. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam (1931-2015) was an Indian scientist and politician, renowned for his significant contributions to India's space and missile programs. Known as the "Missile Man of India," he played a pivotal role in the development of the Indian satellite launch vehicle and the country's nuclear capabilities. Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, Kalam pursued aerospace engineering and worked with the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO).
